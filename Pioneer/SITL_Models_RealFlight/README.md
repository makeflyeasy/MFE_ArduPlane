* Wing span: .......... 3.20 m
* Length: ............. 1.68 m
* Weight: ............. 24.00 kg
* Battery: ............ 12SLiPo - 60000 mAh
* Cruise Power: ....  MFE 6525 kv180 ESC12160 APC2113
* Rotor Power: .......MFE 6518 kv200 ESC12100 MFE2278
